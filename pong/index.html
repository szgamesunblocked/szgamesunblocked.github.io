How to Play Pong the Game?

Playing Pong is easy to learn but hard to master. The game begins with two paddles and a ball. Each player controls a paddle, aiming to hit the ball back and forth across the screen.

The controls are simple: use the arrow keys or mouse to move your paddle up and down. Your goal is to keep the ball in play and prevent it from passing your paddle. Scoring a point occurs when the ball passes your opponent’s paddle and reaches the edge of their side of the screen.

The gameplay of Pong the game is fast-paced, requiring quick reflexes and strategic thinking. As the game progresses, the ball speeds up, making it more challenging to keep up. This increase in difficulty keeps the game exciting and engaging.
